👥 Support Center

Find answers to common questions about domain registration, transfers, and management.

How do I register a domain? +
Search for your desired domain name above, then click "Register Now" on any available domain. You'll be taken to Namecheap's secure checkout where you can complete your purchase.
How long does it take for a domain to go live? +
Most domains activate within minutes via Namecheap's instant activation system. DNS propagation typically takes 1–24 hours to fully propagate worldwide.
Is WHOIS privacy really free? +
Yes! All domains registered via DomHub through Namecheap include free WHOIS privacy protection, keeping your personal information off the public WHOIS database.
Can I transfer my existing domain to DomHub? +
Absolutely. Use the Transfer option in the Domains menu. You'll need your domain's EPP/Auth code from your current registrar. The process takes 5–7 days.
What payment methods are accepted? +
Namecheap accepts Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, Bitcoin, and more. All payments are processed securely on Namecheap's PCI-compliant platform.
What is the refund policy? +
Namecheap offers a refund within 5 days of registration for most domains. Some TLDs (like .io, .ai) may have different policies. Check Namecheap's refund policy for details.
